About Jinna Store

Your trusted partner for quality household products and daily essentials since 2010

Our Story

Jinna Store began as a small neighborhood shop with a simple mission: to provide high-quality household products at affordable prices. Founded in 2010 by Samshudeen, our store has grown from a single location to become a trusted name in household essentials throughout the community.

What started as a modest venture with just a few products has now expanded to offer hundreds of items that families need every day. Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our core values - quality products, fair prices, and personalized service.

Our Mission

At Jinna Store, our mission is to make daily living easier and more convenient for every household we serve. We carefully select each product in our inventory to ensure it meets our high standards for quality, durability, and value.

We believe that everyone deserves access to well-made household essentials without paying premium prices. That's why we work directly with manufacturers and suppliers to bring you the best products at the most competitive prices.
